I grew up in Amherst NY and graduated from Sweet Home High School in 1996. Afterward I attended the State University of New York at Buffalo. I have 2 kids, 1 boy 10 years old and 1 girl 8 years old. I have 3 dogs named Hagrid, Capt Jack, and Boomer.

Over the years I have lived at 8 different duty stations. My favorite duty station was Heidleberg Germany. I love to travel so while overseas I made the most of my time and visited Ireland, Italy, France and Austria. Before having children I worked for the Defense Finance and Account Service and Defense Military Pay Office.

I enjoy watching football, listening to all kinds of music, cooking, watching movies, playing video games and lots of outdoor activities including walking my dogs and hiking.
